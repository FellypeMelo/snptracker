# SNPTracker Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Concise & Technical:** Documentation and mutation reports must be short, informative, and neutral. Focus on technical accuracy and data delivery without unnecessary fluff.

## UX Principles
- **Data-First Layout:** Prioritize high-density data readability and structured tabular layouts for variant information.
- **Minimalist Design:** Maintain a clean interface with zero distractions, ensuring the user can focus entirely on the genetic data.
- **Visual Insights:** Supplement data tables with clear visual charts and sequence maps to help users identify mutation patterns at a glance.

## Brand Identity
- **Modern Biotech (Dark):** The visual aesthetic should feature deep dark backgrounds with high-contrast biological green and blue accents, reflecting a sophisticated, modern scientific tool.

## Accessibility
- **General Accessibility:** Adhere to standard web accessibility guidelines (WCAG AA) to ensure the tool is usable by as many people as possible.
